Who we are

AqlChem is an India-based supplier and manufacturer of chemical and craft-grade raw materials. Nearly three decades in, we work with cosmetic brands, pharma buyers, and independent makers who need materials they can depend on, order after order.

Why buyers stay with us

Consistent batch quality, responsible sourcing, and a support team that treats every order — sample or bulk — the same way. That combination has built a base of over 12,000 repeat customers.

What we supply

Chemicals, herbal extracts, essential oils, colourants, butters & waxes, soap bases, fragrance oils, and clay powders — sourced and tested for cosmetic, pharmaceutical, and personal-use applications.

Essential oils, done right

Lavender for calm, rosemary for haircare, eucalyptus for spa and home use — our oils are pure, undiluted, and graded for cosmetic and aromatherapy use.

Fragrance & soap craft

Long-lasting fragrance oils and melt-and-pour soap bases in glycerine, goat milk, and shea butter variants, built for makers who want to customise scent, texture, and finish.
